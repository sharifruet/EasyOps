# Product & Catalog Management System

## ðŸ“‹ Overview

The Product & Catalog Management System provides a comprehensive, intelligent, and scalable solution for managing product information, pricing, inventory, and catalog operations across multiple sales channels. This system enables businesses to efficiently manage their product portfolio, optimize pricing strategies, and maintain accurate inventory data while providing exceptional customer experiences.

### Key Objectives
- **Comprehensive Product Management**: Complete lifecycle management from product creation to discontinuation
- **Intelligent Pricing**: AI-powered dynamic pricing and promotional strategies
- **Real-time Inventory**: Accurate, real-time inventory tracking across all channels
- **Multi-Channel Synchronization**: Seamless product data synchronization across all sales channels
- **Advanced Analytics**: Data-driven insights for product performance and optimization
- **Scalable Architecture**: Cloud-native design supporting high-volume product catalogs

### System Capabilities
- **Product Information Management**: Centralized product data with rich attributes and specifications
- **Dynamic Pricing Engine**: Flexible pricing models with automated price optimization
- **Inventory Management**: Real-time stock tracking with predictive analytics
- **Catalog Syndication**: Multi-channel product data distribution
- **Performance Analytics**: Comprehensive product performance insights
- **Compliance Management**: Regulatory compliance and certification tracking

## ðŸ”¹ 1. Product Catalog Management

### Basic Information
- **Core Product Details**:
  - Product ID (unique, auto-generated with configurable format)
  - SKU (Stock Keeping Unit, user-defined with validation rules)
  - Barcode / QR Code (multiple barcode support)
  - Product Name (multi-language support)
  - Short Description (SEO-optimized summaries)
  - Full / Rich Description (HTML / WYSIWYG editor support)
  - Product Type (simple, variant, configurable, digital, service, subscription, rental)
  - Category / Subcategory (hierarchical category structure)
  - Brand / Manufacturer Brand
  - Model Number / Part Number
  - HS Code / Tax Code (harmonized system codes)
  - Product Lifecycle Status (active, inactive, draft, discontinued, archived)

### Advanced Product Classification
- **Enhanced Categorization**:
  - Product Family / Group (logical product groupings)
  - Seasonality (summer, winter, all-year, festive, seasonal)
  - Department / Industry Classification (B2B, B2C, industrial)
  - Tags / Keywords (searchable metadata)
  - Custom Attributes (color, material, grade, style, dimensions)
  - Variant Options (size, color, weight, packaging type)
  - Product Relationships (complementary, alternative, upgrade)
  - Cross-Selling / Up-selling Recommendations

### Supplier & Manufacturer Information
- **Supply Chain Details**:
  - Primary Supplier ID / Information
  - Alternate Supplier IDs (backup suppliers)
  - Manufacturer ID / Name / Contact Information
  - Country of Origin (with import/export details)
  - Lead Time (supplier delivery time in days)
  - Warranty / Guarantee Information (terms and conditions)
  - Compliance Certificates (ISO, CE, FDA, UL, etc.)
  - Import/Export Codes (customs classification)
  - Supplier Performance Metrics (delivery, quality ratings)
  - Cost Price / Landing Cost (supplier pricing)

### Stock & Packaging Details
- **Inventory Configuration**:
  - UOM (Unit of Measure - piece, kg, liter, box, etc.)
  - Packaging Type (box, carton, bottle, pallet, bulk)
  - Units per Pack (inner packaging)
  - Packs per Carton (outer packaging)
  - Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ requirements)
  - Reorder Level (automatic reorder triggers)
  - Maximum Stock Level (storage capacity limits)
  - Shelf Life (perishable goods with expiry tracking)
  - Expiry / Best Before Date (if applicable)
  - Batch/Lot Tracking Enabled (traceability requirements)
  - Serial Number Tracking (for high-value items)

### Product Lifecycle Management
- **Lifecycle Tracking**:
  - Launch Date (product introduction date)
  - Last Updated Date (revision tracking)
  - Product Owner (employee ID / department / role)
  - Version Control (product specification versions)
  - Change Management (approval workflows for changes)
  - Product Review Cycles (regular review schedules)
  - End-of-Life Planning (discontinuation timeline)
  - Successor Product Mapping (replacement products)

### Rich Media & Documentation
- **Content Management**:
  - Product Images (multiple high-resolution images)
  - Product Videos (demonstration, tutorial content)
  - 3D Models / AR Content (augmented reality support)

  - User Manuals / Documentation (PDF, online help)
